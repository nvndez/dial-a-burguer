Burguer Express

“Burger Express: Dial-A-Burger” is an interactive 3D platformer game that transports players to the bustling heart of a vibrant city. Here, a small but ambitious owner of a burger food truck, named “Dial-A-Burger,” aspires to turn his business into the culinary sensation of the metropolis.

Armed with enthusiasm and a nimble motorbike, the player uses nearby phone booths to receive orders, collects the irresistible burgers from the food truck, and delivers these delights to customers scattered across the city.

As the player progresses through the game levels, the number of orders increases while the time to deliver them decreases. The player must manage their time well and make precise decisions to complete all tasks before the time runs out. This includes choosing the best routes and deciding how many orders to carry at once.

#1 The Start - Gear Up Your Character

Begin your journey at the “Dial-A-Burger” food truck, already seated on your trusty motorbike. The bustling city awaits as you prepare to take on the role of the city’s favorite burger delivery rider.

#2 The Call - Answering the Order

Hear the ring of a phone booth near the food truck. Ride over quickly to answer the call and receive the customer’s order. Be prepared, as this is just the first of many!

#3 The Pickup - Collecting the Burgers

Return to the food truck to collect the freshly made burgers. Make sure you’re quick and efficient, as the customers are eagerly waiting for their delicious meals.

#4 The Delivery - Racing to the Customer

Navigate the busy streets of the city to deliver the burgers to the customer’s doorstep. Once the delivery is made, keep an ear out for the next ringing phone booth signaling another order. Repeat the process to keep the city’s hunger at bay and your business booming.

Getting Started

Level 1: The First Rush

  • Time Limit: 20 minutes
  • Deliveries: 20 orders
  • Description: Familiarize yourself with the basics of burger delivery. Answer the phone, pick up the orders from the food truck, and deliver them to customers’ homes efficiently.

Level 2: Picking Up the Pace

  • Time Limit: 18 minutes
  • Deliveries: 20 orders
  • Description: With slightly less time on the clock, improve your speed and accuracy. The city’s demands are increasing, and so should your efficiency.

Level 3: The Challenge Begins

  • Time Limit: 15 minutes
  • Deliveries: 20 orders
  • Description: The orders keep coming in. Maintain your cool and streamline your process to keep up with the growing demand.
